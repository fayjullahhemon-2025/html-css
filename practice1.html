Level 1 – Easy (Recall)

Q1. Which tag creates the largest heading?

Answer: <h1> tag creates the largest heading. 

Example: 
 Code:<h1> heading 1 </h1> 
 Output: 
heading 1

Q2. Which tag creates a paragraph?

Answer: <p> tag creates a paragraph. 

Example: 
 Code:<p> Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod
	tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua. </p> 
 Output: 
Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod
	tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.

Q3. What is the difference between strong and b tag

Answer: <strong> and <b> both tags creates bold text and uses for text formatting in html. but <strong> is meaningful and seo friendly. 

Example: 
 Code:<strong> Lorem ipsum </strong> 
 Output: Lorem ipsum 

Example: 
 Code:<b> Lorem ipsum </b> 
 Output: Lorem ipsum

 Output: Google.com

Q6. Which tag creates unordered list?

Answer: <ul> tag creates a unordered list. 

Example: 
 Code:

<li>Item2</li>
<li>Item3</li>
</ul> 
 Output: 
  • Item1
  • Item2
  • Item3
